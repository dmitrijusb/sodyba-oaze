Sodybos Nuoma

For those who value connection to nature above all else

Pabėk iš miesto

Peace Cabin is private waterfront on Ucluelet inlet, on a large lot of old-growth trees. We designed it quite differently from other places you may have stayed-this is a capsule to recharge yourself from the busyness of your day-to-day life.

Mėgaukis pramogomis

The sitting deck features expansive views of the forest and inlet beyond, a gas grill, and a wood fireplace. Rolling cedar shutters keep the rain out and allow sunshine in. Lots of space for your water toys, SUPs, surfboards, kayak, or canoe, as well as a wetsuit drying area outside. Of course, there's a comfortable hot outdoor shower, fully stocked, to top off your stay after a day of exploring the hiking trails, or an afternoon in the surf.
